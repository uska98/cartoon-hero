About Scorpia

Scorpia, from She-Ra and the Princesses of Power, is a princess and Force Captain from the former Scorpion Kingdom, which is now know as the Fright Zone.
When Hordak first appeared on Etheria, the Scorpion Kingdom was the only welcoming kingdom.
Unfortunately, that led to the Scorpion Kingdom being destroyed and the Horde and Fright Zone being built in its place.
Being the only surviving member of the Scorpion Kingdom, Scorpia was raised in the Fright Zone as a Horde solider.
